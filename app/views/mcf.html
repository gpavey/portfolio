March Conservation Fund


WEBSITE

The March Conservation Fund did not have a web presense and was looking for a way to be more visible to the public. They also needed a site where organizations could apply for grant status. It's a great organization and I really enjoyed working with their staff and creating their web presense.


VISIT WEBSITE
THE PROBLEM

Many of the people using the site are in South America and they wanted a site that looked new and fresh but would work on older browsers. They also needed a password protected area for organizations to download files.

THE SOLUTION

I made sure that the javascript used would work well with older browsers and tested it extensively. The password protection was easy with a wp plugin.
