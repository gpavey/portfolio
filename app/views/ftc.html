For The Cause


IDENTITY, WEBSITE

For The Cause was a project built for grassroots organizations. It was designed to be a simple way to find who your local politicians are, their voting record and where their campaign finances were coming from.


THE PROBLEM

It's not very easy to figure out who your local constituents are and if they might be sympathetic to your cause. The data is out there but it's spread out on many different databases. The challenge was to make it all come together in an understandable way.

THE SOLUTION

I wanted a clean look and feel to the website. I wanted it easy to use with no external noise to get in the way of the data. Multiple API calls were used to different databases. That data was then manipulated and fed into the the graphing software I used on the last page.
